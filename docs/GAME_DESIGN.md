# FEDGE 2.O — Game Design Document

## Vision
An engaging mobile game that turns credit education into an adventure. Players feel empowered, not lectured.

## Core Loop
1. **Learn** — Complete a short lesson (2–3 min) about a credit concept
2. **Apply** — Take on a mission that simulates a real credit decision
3. **Earn** — Gain XP, FEDGE Coins, and watch your in-game credit score rise
4. **Repeat** — Daily challenges keep players coming back

## Learning Modules

### Module 1: Credit Score Basics
- What is a credit score?
- The 5 factors (payment history, utilization, age, mix, new credit)
- Score ranges and what they mean

### Module 2: Payment History (35%)
- Why on-time payments are king
- What happens when you miss a payment
- Setting up autopay strategies

### Module 3: Credit Utilization (30%)
- The 30% rule and why it matters
- Balance transfers and paydown strategies
- Utilization vs. credit limits

### Module 4: Credit Age (15%)
- Why older accounts matter
- Which cards to keep open
- Authorized user strategy

### Module 5: Credit Mix (10%)
- Types of credit (revolving, installment, mortgage)
- When to diversify

### Module 6: New Credit (10%)
- Hard vs. soft inquiries
- Rate shopping windows
- Strategic application timing

### Module 7: Credit Repair
- Disputing errors on your credit report
- Pay-for-delete strategies
- Goodwill letter tactics

## Scoring System

| Action | XP | FEDGE Coins |
|--------|----|-------------|
| Complete lesson | +50 | +10 |
| Pass quiz (100%) | +100 | +25 |
| Daily challenge | +75 | +15 |
| 7-day streak | +200 | +50 |
| Module complete | +300 | +100 |

## Progression
- **Level 1–5**: Credit Newbie — learning the basics
- **Level 6–15**: Credit Builder — applying strategies
- **Level 16–25**: Credit Repairer — fixing and rebuilding
- **Level 26–40**: Credit Master — optimization mode
- **Level 41–50**: FEDGE Elite — perfect credit strategies
